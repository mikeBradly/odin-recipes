Garlic Caper Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 pork roast
  • 3/4lb of carrot
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 1 tsp of each: garlic powder, onion powder, parsley and smoked paprika
  • 1/2 lb sweet potato
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 tbsp of fig spread
  • 1/2 lb of brussels sprouts
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp of balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. Bring honey to room temperature.
  2. Place oven rack in center of oven and set to 450 F.
  3. Wash and dry the fresh produce.
  4. Halve the sweet potato then cut crosswise in 1/4" pieces.
  5. Cut off and discard brussels sprouts stems, then cut in half lengthwise.
  6. Peel the carrots, half lengthwise and cut crosswise into 2 inch pieces.
  7. Place all produce in a bowl, cover in spice blend and olive oil then toss.
  8. Arrange all veg on a sheet tray evenly.
  9. Pat the pork dry with paper towel
  10. Transfer pork to bowl, drizzle with olive oil and season with spice blend salt and pepper.
  11. Transfer to center of the sheet tray.
  12. Roast pork and vegetables for about 25 to 27 min.
  13. Peel and finely chop shallot, place in large bowl, add vinegar and drizzle of olive oil
  14. Mix fig spread, honey and mustard in separate bowl
  15. Remove food from oven, toss vegetables in balsamic, shallot marinade
  16. Rest pork for 5 minutes, then slice crosswise and top with honey fig sauce
  17. Split into two portions and serve.
