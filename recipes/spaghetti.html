Quick Hot Dog Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 hot dogs
  • 112 grams of spaghetti
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 chili pepper
  • Red wine
  • Dash of cream
  • Garlic, salt, pepper

Method

  1. Boil 2 hot dogs
  2. Measure out 112 grams of spaghetti
  3. Remove the pit from both tomatoes
  4. Cut of the stem from one chili pepper
  5. Put a dash of red wine, a spoon full of minced garlic both tomatoes and the pepper in a blender
  6. Grab a pan, put a spoon full of oil in it and put it over medium heat
  7. Cut the hotdog into bite sized pieces
  8. Season hotdog with rosemary sage oregano basil and thyme
  9. After browned, add the pasta sauce and water to thin it out
  10. Stir to combine then add the pasta and don't stir for 4 minutes
  11. Continuously add water and cook down sauce and noodle until spaghetti is done
  12. Taste and season with salt and pepper, add cream if the sauce is too spicy
  13. Portion meal onto two plates and enjoy
