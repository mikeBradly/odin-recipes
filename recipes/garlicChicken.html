Garlic Caper Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 lemon
  • 1.5 tsp of chile paste
  • 4 oz orzo pasta
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 tablespoon of capers
  • 2 tablespoons of creme fraiche

Method

  1. Fill a medium pot 3/4th with salted water and heat to boil.
  2. Halve teh zucchini lengthwise, then thinly slice crosswise.
  3. Peel and roughly chop the garlic.
  4. Roughly chop the capers.
  5. Quarter and deseed the lemon.
  6. Add pasta to pot of boiling water, cook for 9 minutes.
  7. In a medium pan, cook the zucchini in 2 tsp olive oil.
  8. When zucchini is lightly browned add half the chopped garlic and chile paste, thoroughly combine, remove from pan.
  9. Dry the chicken and put in bowl
  10. Add to a bowl along with drizzle of olive oil, oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Thoroughly coat.
  11. In the same pan used for zucchini, cook the chicken on medium high heat, 6 to 7 minutes per side, remove from pan.
  12. In the pan of reserved fond, heat drizzel of olive oil on medium high heat
  13. Add capers and remaining choppped garlic and juice of two lemon wedges, remove from heat
  14. To the pasta, add zucchini, creme fraiche, juice of remaining lemon wedges and chopped chicken and garlic caper topping
  15. split into two portions and serve
